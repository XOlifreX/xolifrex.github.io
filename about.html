About me

Hi! I'm Olivier Luyckx, a programmer with a fondness for reverse engineering,
video game preservation, Mario Kart & Japanese.
Did I say Mario Kart already?

Born in 1996, I currently live in Kapellen, Belgium. In 2018 I gratuated from Karel de Grote
College University in Application Development and have found myself doing
this as my daily job. To stay fit I swim twice a week, though I should probably
do more than this.

I like to spend my time in front of my desktop programming and playing video games.
When I'm programming, ofen it's about something video game related, from reverse
engineering GBA/Wii games to making mods/hacks for them. I'm particularly interested in decompilation projects, where you translate the static game binary asm code to C code to
get, in the long term, a new readable, modifyable source code that will compile the original
game. By doing this you'll disect the whole game and get to know literally everything about its inner workings and what every single byte represents. This is especially cool because this
makes it possible to compile that game not for its original platform but to any platform you
want! (Wii, GBA, PS5, PC, ...) In my eyes, this is the ultimate way to preserve video games, which
I'm a die-hard fan of. Ever since the Nintendo Leaks of 2020 I became fully invested in game preservation. Outside of this reversing stuff I have an interest in game development,
specifically about graphics programming. Currently I'm learning OpenGL/Vulkan and hope to master them to one day make my own games.

When I'm not being productive, I like to play video games. As I mentioned, I'm a big Mario Kart fan, more specifically the Wii version.
When people introduce me to others, Mario Kart is usually said within the same breath!
I play in a semi-professional online team called Avail where we participate in online
tournaments in the highest division. When I'm not playing tournaments, I play the Time Trail
mode where I aim to achieve times that will fall in the European/Worldwide Top10.
I still have many goals left, so I will be doing this for quite a while, haha!
Currently I'm ranked #1 of Belgium in Mario Kart Wii, and was Belgian Mario Kart Champion
for a few years officially recognized by Nintendo Benelux, which I'm particularly proud of!
Maybe one day I'll get that title back. :-)
