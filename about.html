About me

Hi! I'm Olivier Luyckx, a programmer with a fondness for reverse engineering, system emulation, video game preservation, Japan & Mario Kart.

Born in 1996, I live in Kapellen, Belgium. In 2018 I gratuated from the Karel de Grote College University in Applied Computer Science - Application Developement and have found myself doing this as my daily job.

You can find my cv for a more professional view here.

In my free time I like to spend time gaming, browsing the internet, watch anime, learn Japanese and coding. When I'm coding in my free time, it's often something video game related. Currently I'm having a big interest in decompilation projects like the projects from the pret community. Because of that? On my Github I started two GBA decompilation projects, one for Mario Kart Advance and one for Hamtatro Ham Ham Games, though I'm not active on them. Outside of reverse engineering and the likes, I have an interest in game development, specifically about graphics programming. Currently I'm learning OpenGL/Vulkan and hope to master them to one day make my own games.

I love Mario Kart, you can find everything about me and Mario Kart on this page.
